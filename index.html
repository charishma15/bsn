About Us

BSN Life Sciences Pvt Ltd incorporated in 2021, are a manufacturer and supplier of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) and Intermediate for a wide range of therapeutic categories. The therapeutic segments of focus are Anti-Fungal, Anti-Depressant, and Anti-Migraine. Anti-Hypertensive, Analgesic, Anti-Histaminic, Antibiotic, and Nootropic and etc.

BSN Life Sciences manufacturing facilities are in Visakhapatnam. The company has a strong presence in domestic market.
BSN Life Sciences continue to reinforce its position as a major strategic partner by creating exceptional value to its customers in the Domestic market.

Underlying this success is our core philosophy of:

  • Offering consistent high-quality products
  • Having a blend of technical knowledge and experience
  • Maintaining organizational strength through unity and transparency
  • Improving the infrastructure with continued capital investment
  • Efficiently handling clients with diverse background from access the india

BSN Life Sciences Pvt Ltd was inaugurated in the year 2021, with a glorious mission and splendid vision of being the “most selected supply collaborator to pharmaceutical clients throughout”. As management team we focus to attain this vision and this mission with a extremely clear focus on quality, economically effective as well as efficient at BSN Life Sciences Private Limited. We team have strong dedication and loyalty of accommodating quality products is possessed by thoroughly manufacturing, production company knowledge, well-qualified team of pros, as well as sophisticated and more improved infrastructure and as a outcome, produced mutualist useful associations for all the parties immersed.

 

BSN Life Sciences is building on the strength of vertical integration in research, process, API manufacturing and intermediates also.
