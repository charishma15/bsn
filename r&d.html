R&D

BSN life sciences established R&D in 2020, has a team of dedicated and well qualified scientists with an experience ranging from 2 to 10 years. The team has a Technical advisor with an experience of 14 years whose expertise in synthetic and as well as process R&D. The labs are well equipped with reactor capacity ranging from 0.25 lts to 25 lits and consist of Auto clave and Rotary evaporator.
